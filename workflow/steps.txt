Steps:
. Collect data
. Preprocessing: Quality control and Trimming
. Remove redundant genes: CD-HIT
. Gene Prediction: metaGeneMark
. Quantification: Salmon
. Binning: MetaBAT 2
. Identification, Annotation and Visualisation of gene clusters: SMASH
. Taxonomic and functional information: MEGAN
. Data analysis and visualization: R packages
